Open Refine


Open Refine is an open source tool to clean and transform your data, an important preparation step before research data can be analysed. In this workshop participants will have hands-on experience using Open Refine to edit a spreadsheet file with simple errors, looking at the role of clustering, faceting, and searching when editing data. This workshop assumes no prior knowledge of the skills or tools.
